The Complete Guide

Olympias Athena Houseboats

Everything you need to know before booking a stay on Dal Lake, Srinagar — rooms, location, food, and how it all works.

Olympias Athena is a family-run, owner-operated houseboat property on Dal Lake, Srinagar — two boats, Olympias and Athena, moored at one of the lake's most established addresses, run personally by owner Saif ul Rehman Guroo for over 15 years. This guide covers everything a first-time booker needs to know.

📍 Location

Olympias Athena sits at Ghat No. 13, Boulevard Road, Dal Lake, Srinagar, Jammu & Kashmir 190001 — a sheltered, well-established stretch of the lake with clear views of the Zabarwan range.

  • From Srinagar Airport: ~14–16 km, about 30–40 minutes by road
  • From Srinagar Railway Station: ~12–14 km, about 25–35 minutes by road
  • Access from the ghat is by Shikara — arranged as part of your booking, no separate transfer needed

🛶 The Two Houseboats: Olympias & Athena

Named for Olympias, mother of Alexander the Great, and her daughter Athena, the two boats sit side by side and share the same standard of hand-carved walnut craftsmanship, hospitality, and included amenities. Between them, the property offers 15+ rooms, making it suitable for solo travelers, couples, and larger family or group bookings that need multiple rooms under one roof.

✨ What's Included

  • Unlimited Shikara rides — floating gardens, sunset routes, and local markets, no extra charge
  • Hand-carved walnut interiors — ceilings, bed frames, and window lattices, done by local artisans
  • Sundeck access — for morning tea, evening meals, or simply watching the lake
  • Direct owner hospitality — questions answered by Saif ul Rehman Guroo himself, not a call center
  • Wi-Fi and modern comforts — balanced with the traditional houseboat aesthetic

🍽️ Food & Wazwan

Authentic Kashmiri Wazwan — Rogan Josh, Dum Aloo, and other traditional dishes — is available as an optional meal plan, cooked fresh rather than pre-made. Portions and spice levels can be adjusted for children or dietary needs with advance notice.
