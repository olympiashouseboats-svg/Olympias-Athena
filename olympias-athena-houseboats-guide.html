About Olympias Athena Group of Houseboats

Experiences Included with Your Stay

🛶 Complimentary Shikara Rides

Every guest at Olympias Athena enjoys complimentary Shikara rides on Dal Lake. Glide through calm waters at sunrise, explore floating gardens, visit the floating vegetable market, and watch the sunset paint the mountains gold — all included with your booking. Our experienced Shikara pilots will take you on memorable journeys across the lake.

🍽️ Authentic Kashmiri Cuisine

Savor traditional Kashmiri Wazwan cuisine prepared fresh by our experienced local chefs. Menu highlights include:

  • Rogan Josh (slow-cooked lamb in aromatic gravy)
  • Yakhni (yogurt-based mutton curry)
  • Gushtaba (minced meatballs in rich gravy)
  • Tabak Maaz (fried lamb ribs)
  • Kashmiri Pulao and Saffron Rice
  • Fresh Kahwa (traditional Kashmiri green tea)
  • Nadru (lotus stem) delicacies

Vegetarian, Jain, and continental options are also available. All meals are prepared with locally sourced ingredients and can be customized to your dietary preferences.

🏔️ Sightseeing & Local Tours

While staying at Olympias Athena, you're perfectly positioned to explore Srinagar's top attractions:

  • Mughal Gardens: Shalimar Bagh, Nishat Bagh, Chashme Shahi (15-20 minutes away)
  • Hazratbal Shrine: Beautiful lakeside mosque (10 minutes)
  • Shankaracharya Temple: Hilltop temple with panoramic city views (20 minutes)
  • Old City & Markets: Lal Chowk, Polo View, handicraft shops (15 minutes)
  • Day Trips: Gulmarg (2 hours), Pahalgam (2.5 hours), Sonamarg (3 hours)

Our team can arrange taxis, guided tours, and customized itineraries at competitive rates. Just let us know your interests and we'll handle the logistics.
