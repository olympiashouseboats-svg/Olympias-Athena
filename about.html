Why Kashmir · Dal Lake Srinagar Tourism

Why Dal Lake is Kashmir's Crown Jewel

Dal Lake in Srinagar, Jammu & Kashmir is one of the most iconic natural destinations in all of Asia. Spread across 18 square kilometres in the heart of the Kashmir Valley, surrounded by the majestic Zabarwan Mountains and the snow-capped Himalayas, Dal Lake is — quite simply — paradise on earth.

The lake is a living ecosystem — home to floating gardens (Rad), lotus fields, ancient Mughal terraced gardens (Nishat Bagh, Shalimar Bagh), the sacred Hazratbal Shrine, and the iconic Shikara boat culture that Olympias Athena guests experience from Ghat No.13, Boulevard Road, Srinagar.

  • ✦ Named one of Asia's most beautiful lakes
  • ✦ UNESCO-recognised cultural landscape
  • ✦ Home to the world-famous Kashmiri houseboat tradition
  • ✦ Gateway to Gulmarg, Pahalgam, Sonamarg, and beyond
  • ✦ Year-round destination with millions of annual visitors
