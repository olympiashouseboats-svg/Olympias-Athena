Who We Are · Srinagar Kashmir

A Legacy of Kashmiri Warmth on Dal Lake

Olympias Athena Group of Houseboats is a family-owned luxury houseboat business nestled at Ghat No. 13, Boulevard Road, Dal Lake, Srinagar — one of the most iconic addresses in all of Jammu & Kashmir. We are not just a place to stay; we are a doorway into the soul of Kashmir.

Founded with a deep passion for authentic Kashmiri hospitality, Olympias Athena Group of Houseboats has been welcoming guests from across India and the world to experience the timeless beauty of Dal Lake. Our two beautifully crafted houseboats feature 15+ luxury rooms — each adorned with intricate hand-carved walnut woodwork, papier-mâché ceilings, and furnishings that reflect centuries of Kashmiri master craftsmanship.

Whether you arrive as a tourist eager to experience Kashmir's legendary beauty, a couple seeking romance on the shimmering water, a family creating lifelong memories, or a local guest who simply wants to experience Dal Lake — we welcome every guest as our own. Our warm team, led personally by owner Saif ul Rehman Guroo, ensures that every stay is personalised, peaceful, and unforgettable.

The Heart Behind Olympias

Saif ul Rehman Guroo grew up on the shores of Dal Lake, Srinagar — raised in the same tradition of Kashmiri hospitality that his family has practised for generations. His deep love for Dal Lake and genuine passion for welcoming guests is the soul of Olympias Athena Group of Houseboats.

Saif personally oversees every guest's experience — from arranging airport pickups and customising meals to organising private Shikara rides at sunrise. He believes that true hospitality means treating every guest not as a customer, but as a cherished visitor to his home.

Under his leadership, Olympias Athena Group of Houseboats has earned a 4.9-star reputation, with hundreds of guests returning year after year from across India and beyond. When guests arrive, Saif is there to greet them. When they leave, they leave as family.

Why Kashmir · Srinagar Tourism

Why Dal Lake is Kashmir's Crown Jewel

Dal Lake in Srinagar, Jammu & Kashmir is one of the most iconic natural destinations in all of Asia. Spread across 18 square kilometres in the heart of the Kashmir Valley, surrounded by the majestic Zabarwan Mountains and the snow-capped Himalayas, Dal Lake is — quite simply — paradise on earth.

The lake is a living ecosystem — home to floating gardens (Rad), lotus fields, ancient Mughal terraced gardens (Nishat Bagh, Shalimar Bagh), the sacred Hazratbal Shrine, and the iconic Shikara boat culture. Millions of visitors from across India and the world visit Srinagar every year to experience this extraordinary place.

  • ✦ Named as one of Asia's most beautiful lakes
  • ✦ UNESCO-recognised cultural landscape
  • ✦ Home to the world-famous Kashmiri houseboat tradition
  • ✦ Gateway to Gulmarg, Pahalgam, Sonamarg, and beyond
  • ✦ Year-round destination for tourists across India
