Your Honeymoon Story on Dal Lake

You arrive at Boulevard Road and board a Shikara that carries you across Dal Lake to Olympias Athena. The houseboat is decorated with marigold garlands and rose petals for your arrival. Saif welcomes you with Kahwa and dried fruits while your room is revealed — king bed draped with rose petals, the lake filling your window, the mountains beyond wrapped in evening gold.

That evening, as the sun sets over Dal Lake, your private Shikara is waiting. Just the two of you, your boatman, and the most beautiful light on Earth — the whole lake turning amber and rose as the day ends. Back at the houseboat, a candlelit Wazwan dinner awaits on the sundeck: multiple courses of Kashmir's finest cuisine, stars emerging overhead, the lake quiet and still below.

Every morning of your stay begins with sunrise breakfast on the sundeck — fresh bread, eggs, Kashmiri chai — and the option of an early Shikara ride to the floating flower market before other tourists wake. These are the moments that become the stories you tell for the rest of your lives together.

"We spent our honeymoon at Olympias Athena and it was the most romantic experience of our lives. The rose petal welcome, the private sunset Shikara, the candlelit dinner on the deck — Saif thought of everything. Waking up to Dal Lake every morning felt like a dream. Kashmir and Olympias Athena will always be our place."

— Priya & Rahul, Mumbai (Honeymoon 2025)
