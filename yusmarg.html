Yusmarg Kashmir - Complete Kashmir Guide

Yusmarg, meaning Meadow of Jesus, is one of Kashmir's best-kept secrets. Located 47 km from Srinagar in the Budgam district at 2,400 meters, this pristine alpine meadow remains far less touristy than Gulmarg or Pahalgam, offering an authentic Kashmir experience in stunning natural surroundings.

Key Information

Distance from Srinagar: 47 km from Srinagar (1.5 hours)

Best Time to Visit: May to October

Ideal For: Nature lovers, photographers, trekkers, families seeking unspoiled Kashmir
