Olympias Houseboat: Premium Dal Lake Accommodation

Olympias houseboat is one of two premium vessels operated under the Olympias Athena brand at Boulevard Road, Ghat No. 13 on Dal Lake Srinagar. Named after the mother of Alexander the Great, Olympias represents regal elegance, authentic Kashmiri craftsmanship, and personalized hospitality that has made it a favorite among discerning travelers visiting Kashmir.

What Makes Olympias Special

Olympias features 8+ luxury rooms across multiple categories, each showcasing museum-quality hand-carved walnut wood interiors, traditional papier-mâché ceilings, and modern amenities. The houseboat maintains perfect balance between heritage authenticity and contemporary comfort.

🏠 Room Options on Olympias

  • Deluxe Rooms: Spacious 250+ sq ft with attached bathrooms, comfortable beds, lake views
  • Premium Lake-View Rooms: Front-facing with panoramic Dal Lake and mountain views
  • Luxury Suites: Most spacious accommodations with separate living areas

✨ Olympias Amenities

  • Hand-carved walnut wood interiors throughout
  • 24/7 hot water in attached bathrooms
  • Complimentary Wi-Fi
  • Room heaters for winter stays
  • Sundeck with mountain views
  • Common dining area
  • Complimentary Shikara rides
  • Authentic Kashmiri Wazwan meals

Booking Olympias Houseboat

When you book with Olympias Athena, owner Saif ul Rehman Guroo may assign you to Olympias houseboat based on availability. Both Olympias and its sister houseboat Athena maintain identical quality standards and services — the assignment simply depends on room availability and your group size.
