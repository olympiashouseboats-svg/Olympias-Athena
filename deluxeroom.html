The Deluxe Room Experience

Wake up in the Deluxe Room to a view that will take your breath away — Dal Lake stretching before you, snow-capped Himalayan peaks glowing in the morning light, and the gentle sound of water lapping against the houseboat. This is your daily view when you stay with us.

The room interior is a testament to centuries-old Kashmiri craftsmanship. Master artisans spend months hand-carving the walnut wood panels that line the walls, while papier-mâché artists apply traditional patterns to the ceiling — a 500-year-old art form unique to Kashmir. Every morning you breakfast on the sundeck overlooking Dal Lake, and every evening you take complimentary Shikara rides as the sun sets behind the Zabarwan mountains.

The attached bathroom features modern fixtures with 24/7 hot water. Clean, comfortable, and private — a welcome contrast to the rustic charm of the room's décor. The bed is made with quality linen and extra blankets are always available for cool Kashmir evenings.
