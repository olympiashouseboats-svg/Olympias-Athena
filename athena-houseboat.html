Athena Houseboat

Athena Houseboat: Luxury Dal Lake Living

Athena houseboat is one of two premium vessels operated under the Olympias Athena brand at Boulevard Road, Ghat No. 13 on Dal Lake Srinagar. Named after the Greek goddess of wisdom and warfare, Athena embodies elegance, traditional Kashmiri artistry, and the personalized care that defines our commitment to exceptional guest experiences in Kashmir.

Athena Houseboat Features

Athena features 8+ premium rooms showcasing the finest Kashmiri craftsmanship — intricate hand-carved walnut wood panels covering walls and ceilings, traditional papier-mâché artwork, and carefully curated furnishings that blend heritage charm with modern comfort.

🛏️ Accommodations on Athena

  • Deluxe Rooms: Comfortable 250+ sq ft with lake views, attached bathrooms, premium bedding
  • Lake-View Suites: Enhanced rooms with panoramic Dal Lake and Zabarwan mountain views
  • Premium Suites: Spacious accommodations with separate seating areas
