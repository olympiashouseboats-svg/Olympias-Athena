Frequently Asked · Dal Lake Houseboat Rooms

Questions About Our Rooms & Stays

What types of houseboat rooms are available at Olympias Athena? +
We offer 6 room types at Olympias Athena Group of Houseboats, Ghat No.13, Dal Lake, Srinagar: Premium Lake View Suite (best for couples/solo), Royal Couple Suite (honeymoon & romance), Grand Family Suite (3–6 guests), Classic Houseboat Room (tourists), Heritage Executive Room (business/solo), and Local Comfort Room (local guests & budget stays). All rooms have a direct Dal Lake view.
Do all rooms have a Dal Lake view? +
Yes — 100% of our rooms at Olympias Athena have a direct Dal Lake view. Every guest wakes up to the shimmering waters of Dal Lake and the Zabarwan Mountain backdrop right from their window or private deck.
Is there a couple room / honeymoon suite on Dal Lake Srinagar? +
Yes — our Royal Couple Suite is designed for honeymoons, anniversaries, and romantic Kashmir escapes. It features carved walnut décor, king-size bed, lake and mountain views, private lakeside seating, and optional candle-lit arrangements on Dal Lake.
What is check-in and check-out time? +
Check-in is at 12:00 noon and check-out is at 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out can be arranged subject to availability — WhatsApp us at +91 9596431735 to confirm.
Are Kashmiri meals included with the room? +
Kashmiri meals — authentic Wazwan, Rogan Josh, Dum Aloo, noon chai, and Kashmiri bread — are available on request. We offer both full-board (room + meals) and room-only packages. WhatsApp us to customise your package.
Where exactly are you located on Dal Lake? +
We are moored at Ghat No.13, Boulevard Road, Dal Lake, Srinagar, Jammu & Kashmir — 190001. It is one of the most central and accessible locations on Dal Lake, walking distance from key Srinagar attractions. Owner Saif ul Rehman Guroo personally greets guests on arrival.
How do I book a houseboat room on Dal Lake Srinagar? +
The fastest way is via WhatsApp at +91 9596431735 or by calling the same number. You can also use our contact form. Saif ul Rehman Guroo personally confirms every booking and arranges your arrival at Ghat No.13, Boulevard Road, Dal Lake.
