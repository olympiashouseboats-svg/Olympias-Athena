Houseboat Selection Strategy

Step-by-Step Selection Process

1. Verify Location Authenticity

Request specific ghat numbers. Boulevard Road properties should have explicit addresses like "Ghat No. 13" not vague "near Boulevard Road" descriptions.

2. Request Current Visual Evidence

Demand photos taken within 3-6 months, not outdated promotional materials. Request video calls to see actual room conditions in real-time.

3. Verify Craftsmanship Quality

Request detailed specifications about wood types, carving techniques, and artisan information. Genuine hand-carved pieces have distinctly different appearances than molded panels.

4. Communicate Directly with Owner

Always speak with the owner/manager before booking. Their communication quality and responsiveness indicate hospitality philosophy.

5. Request Detailed Pricing Breakdown

Insist on all-inclusive quotes with explicit itemization. Verify what's included and confirm no hidden charges exist.

6. Cross-Reference Multiple Reviews

Check Google Reviews, TripAdvisor, and other platforms. Contact previous guests directly if possible. Consistency in reviews indicates reliability.

7. Visit Before Booking if Possible

Many quality houseboats welcome daytime visits. Personal inspection provides confidence in your booking decision.
