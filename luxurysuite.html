The Luxury Suite Experience

Enter the Luxury Suite and you enter a world apart. The private living room — furnished with carved walnut sofa sets, a traditional Kashmiri fireplace-style heater, and hand-knotted silk carpets — is where you begin and end each day. Sip your morning Kahwa here as Dal Lake glows in the first light, and return in the evening for Noon Chai as stars emerge over the mountains.

The bedroom is a sanctuary of calm — a king-size bed with premium linen faces directly onto the lake, so the last thing you see before sleep and the first thing on waking is Dal Lake's mirror-still water. The bathroom features the finest fixtures we offer, with a deep soaking shower and 24/7 hot water always ready.

All three meals are included with the Luxury Suite — breakfast, lunch, and a multi-course Kashmiri Wazwan dinner served privately in your suite or on the sundeck as the sun melts into the Zabarwan mountains. Airport pickup and drop are included as standard. You arrive, we handle everything else.
