Why Families Love Staying With Us

A houseboat on Dal Lake is one of the most memorable experiences you can give your children. Every morning a Shikara arrives to take the family across the lake — children can try paddling, spot kingfishers and herons, and explore the floating gardens where vegetables actually grow on water. This is education and adventure combined in a way no classroom can match.

Our Family Room is thoughtfully arranged to ensure parents have a comfortable double bed while children have their own single beds — all within the same beautifully carved room. The spacious attached bathroom means no morning queue. Meals are generous and family-friendly; our kitchen happily prepares milder options for younger guests who find Kashmiri spices new.

Owner Saif ul Rehman Guroo has hosted hundreds of families and understands what matters: safety, space, good food, and experiences that children will speak about for years. Boulevard Road Ghat 13 is one of the calmest moorings on Dal Lake — the gentle rocking that helps children sleep is never rough enough to cause concern.
