Understanding Houseboat Package Types

Kashmir houseboats are generally booked under three package structures. Picking the right one depends on how much you want to explore Srinagar's restaurants versus enjoying meals onboard.

1. Room-Only (Bed & Breakfast)

Covers your room and breakfast, leaving lunch and dinner flexible. Ideal for travelers who want to sample Srinagar's street food and restaurants like Mughal Darbar or Lhasa.

2. Half-Board (Breakfast + Dinner)

The most popular option. You get breakfast and a home-style Kashmiri dinner on the deck, while lunch stays open for sightseeing days.

3. Full-Board (All Meals Included)

Best for honeymooners and families with kids who prefer not to step out for every meal. All three meals are served fresh onboard, often featuring Rogan Josh, Yakhni, and Kashmiri kahwa.
