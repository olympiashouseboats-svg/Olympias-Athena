Best Time to Visit Kashmir

Kashmir is Beautiful All Year Round

Every season on Dal Lake offers a completely different but equally breathtaking experience. Our houseboat stays are available year-round — plan your Kashmir holiday in the season that calls to you.

🌸

Spring — Mar to May

The world-famous Tulip Garden blooms. Cherry blossoms line Boulevard Road. Dal Lake glitters in warm golden light. The most photographed season in Kashmir.

☀️

Summer — Jun to Aug

Peak tourist season. Cool 24°C weather. Lotus flowers carpet Dal Lake. Shikaras glide through blooms. Perfect escape from the scorching heat of the Indian plains.

🍁

Autumn — Sep to Nov

Golden Chinar trees reflect in the lake. Saffron fields bloom in Pampore. The most romantic season — fiery reds, deep oranges, fewer crowds, and a magical quiet calm.

❄️

Winter — Dec to Feb

Snow blankets the Zabarwan Mountains. Dal Lake freezes at the edges. Our warm, wood-panelled rooms become a cosy sanctuary. A truly rare and unforgettable Kashmir experience.

Getting Here · Srinagar, Kashmir

Finding Us on Dal Lake

Olympias Athena Group of Houseboats is moored at Ghat No.13, Boulevard Road — one of the most accessible and well-known ghats on Dal Lake.

✈️ From Srinagar Airport

Approximately 14–16 km, around 30–40 minutes by road depending on traffic. Prepaid taxis are available at the airport, or let us know your flight details and we can help arrange a pickup.

🚉 From Srinagar Railway Station

Roughly 12–14 km from the station to Boulevard Road, typically a 25–35 minute drive by taxi or auto-rickshaw.

📍 On Boulevard Road

Look for Ghat No.13, near the Passport Office — minutes on foot from Dal Lake gardens, the floating market, and Boulevard Road's restaurants and shikara stands.
