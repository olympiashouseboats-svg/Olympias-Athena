Things to Do Near Dal Lake Srinagar

The Complete Sightseeing Itinerary for Your Houseboat Stay

Kashmir Sightseeing · Dal Lake Srinagar

Make the Most of Your Time Around Dal Lake

A houseboat stay on Dal Lake is the perfect base for exploring Srinagar and beyond. From Mughal-era gardens to bustling Old City markets and snow-capped day trips, here's a practical itinerary to help you plan your days — whether you have 2 days or a full week.

A Shikara Ride at Sunset — One of Dal Lake's Signature Experiences

Right Around Dal Lake

1

Shikara Ride Through the Lake

Glide past floating gardens, the famous floating vegetable market, and quiet backwaters — best at sunrise or sunset.

2

Mughal Gardens — Nishat & Shalimar Bagh

Terraced 17th-century gardens with fountains, Chinar trees, and views over the lake, just a short ride from Boulevard Road.

3

Shankaracharya Temple

Perched on a hilltop, this ancient temple offers panoramic views of Srinagar and Dal Lake below — worth the climb for the vista alone.

4

Old City Markets & Jamia Masjid

Explore narrow lanes of papier-mâché workshops, pashmina shawl traders, and the historic Jamia Masjid with its 370 wooden pillars.

Day Trips Beyond Srinagar

🏔️ Gulmarg (1.5–2 hrs)

Home to one of the world's highest cable cars (Gondola), with meadows in summer and skiing in winter.

🌲 Pahalgam (2–2.5 hrs)

Pine forests, the Lidder River, and Betaab Valley make this a favorite for nature walks and photography.

🏞️ Sonmarg (2.5–3 hrs)

The "Meadow of Gold" offers glacier views and is the gateway to the Zojila Pass — ideal for summer visits.

Sample 4-Day Itinerary

  • Day 1: Arrive, settle into your houseboat, sunset Shikara ride
  • Day 2: Mughal Gardens, Shankaracharya Temple, Old City markets
  • Day 3: Full-day Gulmarg trip with Gondola cable car
  • Day 4: Floating market sunrise tour, leisure morning, departure

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How many days are enough to explore Srinagar from a houseboat?

A: 3 to 4 days lets you cover Mughal Gardens, the Old City, Shankaracharya Temple, Shikara rides, and a day trip to Gulmarg or Pahalgam without rushing.

Q: Can Olympias Athena arrange sightseeing tours?

A: Yes, we arrange taxis, guided tours, and customized itineraries to Mughal Gardens, Old City markets, and day trips to Gulmarg and Pahalgam at competitive rates.

Q: Is Gulmarg a feasible day trip from a Dal Lake houseboat?

A: Yes — Gulmarg is roughly 1.5 to 2 hours by road from Dal Lake, making it an easy day trip for cable car rides and mountain views.
