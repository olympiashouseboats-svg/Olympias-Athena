Vaishno Devi Katra - Complete Kashmir Guide

Vaishno Devi, located in the Trikuta Mountains near Katra in Reasi district, is one of India's most revered Hindu shrines and the country's second-most visited pilgrimage site after Tirupati. The sacred cave temple dedicated to Goddess Vaishno Devi attracts 8-10 million devotees annually, who trek 13-14 km through stunning mountain terrain to seek the deity's blessings.

Key Information

Distance from Srinagar: 315 km from Srinagar (7-8 hours) or 50 km from Jammu

Best Time to Visit: May to October

Ideal For: Nature lovers, photographers, trekkers, families seeking unspoiled Kashmir
